Welcome to Korea GeoGuessr!

Test your knowledge of South Korea by guessing locations from images. The closer your guess, the more points you earn!

1

Create an Account

Sign up to save your scores and compete on the leaderboard. You can also play as a guest, but your scores won't be saved.

2

Start a New Game

Click "Play Now" on the home page to start a new game. Each game consists of 5 rounds in different locations around South Korea.

3

Explore the Location

You'll be shown images from a random location in South Korea. Use the navigation arrows to view different angles and look for clues.

4

Make Your Guess

Click on the map to place your guess pin. Try to be as accurate as possible. When ready, click "Make Guess" to submit.

5

See Your Score

After each round, you'll see how close your guess was to the actual location. Points are awarded based on distance - closer guesses earn more points!

6

Complete All Rounds

After 5 rounds, your total score will be displayed. You can save your score to the leaderboard if you're logged in.

Scoring System

Points are awarded based on how close your guess is to the actual location:

  • 0-1 km: 5000 points
  • 1-5 km: 4000-5000 points
  • 5-10 km: 3000-4000 points
  • 10-50 km: 2000-3000 points
  • 50-100 km: 1000-2000 points
  • 100+ km: 0-1000 points

The maximum possible score for a game is 25,000 points (5,000 per round).
